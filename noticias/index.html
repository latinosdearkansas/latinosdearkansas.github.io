Latinos de Arkansas

Latinos de Arkansas is a tool of massive communication among Latin and Spanish speakers of Hope, Nashville, Texarkana, De Queen, Hot Springs, Glenwood and surrounding areas. We are also expanding our communication throughout the State of Arkansas and the world, using the latest techonogy in communication and social media.
